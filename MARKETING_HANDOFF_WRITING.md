# Marketing Handoff — Newsletter Drafting Feature

**For:** Marketing Claude (alwaysready.uk)
**From:** Platform Claude (alwaysready-inspection-readiness-platform)

---

## What was built

A newsletter drafting tool for care home managers and admins. It uses AI to generate a polished, ready-to-send newsletter draft from a few bullet points. AlwaysReady does not send on behalf of providers — it is a writing aid only.

---

## Audience and access

- Available to **admin** and **manager** logins only — not staff or viewer roles
- 10 drafts per month per organisation, resets on the 1st of each month
- Included in the standard subscription — not a paid add-on

Running a care service means a constant stream of communication — monthly updates to families, policy changes for staff, seasonal messages, inspection announcements. It all takes time you don't have.

The AlwaysReady newsletter drafting tool uses AI to turn a few bullet points into a fully written, professional newsletter in seconds. Just choose your audience, pick a topic, add any key points you want included, and select a tone. A polished, ready-to-use draft appears immediately.

No writing experience needed. No staring at a blank page. Copy the finished text and paste it wherever you need it — email, WhatsApp, printed notice board, or your own system.

AlwaysReady never sends on your behalf. You always read it, approve it, and send it yourself.
