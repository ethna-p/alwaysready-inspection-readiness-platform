## What was built

A self-assessment mock inspection tool for care home admins. It allows a manager to step through their KLOEs — either a full inspection covering all five key questions, or a partial inspection focused on one key question — rating each I statement and KLOE, then receiving a prioritised, forward-looking action plan at the end.

---

## Important disclaimer — must appear in all copy

Mock inspections on AlwaysReady are a **self-assessment tool** only. They do not represent the view of CQC, and AlwaysReady makes no claim about how a CQC inspector would rate the service. CQC inspectors exercise independent professional judgement. This disclaimer must be reflected, in plain English, in any marketing copy about this feature.

Do not write copy that implies providers will know their CQC rating in advance, or that a good mock inspection score means a good CQC inspection outcome.

---

## Audience and access

- Available to **admin** logins only
- Included in the standard subscription — not a paid add-on
- Partial inspections are recommended for providers currently rated Good or above
- Full inspections are recommended for providers rated Requires Improvement or Inadequate

---

## How it works (for your reference — do not copy verbatim)

1. Admin selects full or partial inspection. For partial, they choose a key question (Safe, Effective, Caring, Responsive, or Well-Led).
2. The platform steps through each KLOE in scope, one at a time.
3. For each KLOE, the admin rates every I statement as Met, Partial, or Not Met, then gives the KLOE an overall rating (Outstanding, Good, Requires Improvement, or Inadequate).
4. On completion, the platform generates a report with summary findings by key question and a prioritised action plan in three tiers: Must Address, Strengthen Before Inspection, and Maintain.
5. The action plan is written in plain, forward-looking language — "you need to..." and "we recommend..." — not a list of failures.
6. The report can be printed or saved as a PDF.

### FAQ entries (for the pre-trial FAQ page or Help)

**What is a mock inspection?**
A mock inspection is a self-assessment exercise that steps you through your KLOEs in the same structure a CQC inspector would use. You rate each I statement and KLOE, and AlwaysReady produces a prioritised action plan. It is a guidance tool — it does not predict your CQC rating.

**Should I run a full or partial mock inspection?**
Providers currently rated Good or above typically receive a focused inspection from CQC, covering one or two key questions. If that applies to you, a partial mock inspection is a realistic preparation exercise. Providers rated Requires Improvement or Inadequate are more likely to receive a full inspection, so a full mock is recommended.

**Will AlwaysReady tell me what my CQC rating will be?**
No. Mock inspections are a self-assessment tool only. CQC inspectors exercise independent professional judgement — AlwaysReady cannot predict their conclusions. The ratings produced in a mock inspection reflect your own assessment, not CQC's view.
