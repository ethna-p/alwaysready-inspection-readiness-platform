**Subject:** Three things worth exploring in AlwaysReady
**Trigger:** Day 3 of trial

---

Dear [Name],

Now that you've had a couple of days to settle in, we wanted to highlight three features that our users find particularly valuable.

**The Daily Report** gives you a snapshot of your current compliance position — which KLOEs need attention, what's overdue, and what's looking strong. You'll find it under **Daily Report** in the top navigation. It's designed to be the first thing a manager checks at the start of the day.

**Evidence uploads** allow you to attach documents directly to each KLOE — policies, audits, meeting minutes, certificates. Everything is stored securely and is accessible during an inspection. Open any KLOE under the **KLOEs** tab to find the evidence upload panel.

**The Inspection Pack** generates a single downloadable document summarising your readiness across all KLOEs — useful as a teaching tool for your team or as a reference when preparing for review. You'll find it under **Inspection Pack** in the top navigation.

If you have any questions, we're always happy to help at hello@alwaysready.uk.
