# Day 9 — Did you know

**Subject:** A few things you might not have tried yet
**Trigger:** Day 9 of trial

Dear [Name],

With five days of your trial remaining, we wanted to share a few features that are easy to miss but genuinely useful in practice.

**The Audit Trail** keeps a complete record of every change made to a KLOE — who updated it, when, and what changed. This can be invaluable during an inspection when you need to demonstrate how your compliance position has developed over time. You'll find it under the **Trend** tab in the top navigation.

**Visitor access** allows you to grant a read-only login to an external reviewer, consultant, or responsible individual, without giving them editing rights. Access expires automatically. You'll find this under the **Team** tab.

**The Trend Report** shows your readiness score over time, so you can see at a glance whether your overall compliance position is improving. You'll find it under the **Trend** tab in the top navigation.

**The HR module** keeps your staff records, training certificates, DBS checks, supervision logs, and appraisal records in one place — all accessible during an inspection. CQC inspectors will want to see evidence that your team is properly trained and supported, and the HR module makes that straightforward to demonstrate. You'll find it under the **HR** tab in the top navigation.

If you'd like any guidance on these features, the Support tab inside the platform is the best place to reach us.
