# Week 10 — How we keep your data safe

**Subject:** How AlwaysReady keeps your data safe
**Trigger:** Week 10 after subscription confirmed

Dear [Name],

This week we would like to tell you about how we protect the data you store in AlwaysReady.

We understand that the information you enter into the platform is sensitive. Staff HR records, supervision notes, and compliance documentation are not things that should be left unsecured. Here is what we do to keep your data safe.

All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. This means your information is protected whether it is being stored or moving between your device and our servers. Every file you upload is automatically scanned for viruses and malware before it is stored. Your data is held within the European Union and is not transferred outside of it. Each organisation's data is completely isolated from all other organisations on the platform. Nobody else can see your records.

We take data protection seriously, and we are committed to full compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation. AlwaysReady acts as a data processor on your behalf. You remain the data controller and retain full ownership of your data at all times. A Data Processing Agreement is available on request.

You can read our full Data Security Statement and Data Retention Policy in the Help section inside the platform.

If you have any questions about data security, the Support tab inside the platform is the best place to reach us.

The AlwaysReady Team
