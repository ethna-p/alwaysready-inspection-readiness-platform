# AlwaysReady — Data Retention and Export Policy (Draft)

**Status:** Draft — for legal review before publishing
**Last updated:** July 2026

---

## Overview

AlwaysReady is committed to handling your data responsibly. This policy explains how long we keep your data, what happens to it when your subscription ends, and how you can export it at any time.

---

## How long we keep your data

**While your subscription is active**
Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. This includes all KLOE records, compliance updates, audit trail entries, team member details, and any uploaded evidence files.

**After your subscription ends**
If your subscription lapses or is cancelled, your data is retained for **90 days** from the date your account closes. This gives you time to export your records before they are permanently deleted.

After 90 days, all data associated with your organisation is permanently and irreversibly deleted from our systems. This cannot be undone.

**Trial accounts**
If you do not subscribe at the end of your 90-day free trial, your data is retained for a further **30 days** before being permanently deleted. You will not be notified before deletion occurs, so please export any records you wish to keep before your trial ends.

**Demo accounts**
Demo environments are reset nightly and contain no real data. No data retention applies to demo accounts.

---

## Exporting your data

You can request a full export of your organisation's data at any time by emailing **hello@alwaysready.uk**. Please include your organisation name and the email address associated with your account.

**What is included in a data export:**
- All KLOE records and compliance updates (CSV format)
- Full audit trail (CSV format)
- Team member list (CSV format)
- Any uploaded evidence files (ZIP archive)

We will deliver your export within **5 working days** of receiving your request.

**Note:** Data exports are provided in CSV format, which can be opened in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, or any standard spreadsheet application.

---

## Your rights under GDPR

As a data controller for your organisation's information, you have the right to:

- **Access** your data at any time by requesting an export
- **Rectify** any inaccurate data by updating it directly in the platform
- **Erase** your data by closing your account and allowing the 90-day retention period to elapse, or by requesting immediate deletion by emailing hello@alwaysready.uk
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format (CSV) on request

AlwaysReady acts as a **data processor** on your behalf. You remain the **data controller** for all information entered into the platform.

---

## Requesting early deletion

If you wish your data to be deleted before the 90-day retention period has elapsed, please email **hello@alwaysready.uk** with the subject line "Data deletion request". We will confirm deletion within 5 working days.

Please note that once data is deleted it cannot be recovered.
